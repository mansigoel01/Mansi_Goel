About Mansi Goel

Passionate Ph.D. Scholar exploring how cutting-edge computational methods can revolutionize the food domain.

Academic Background

  • Dual Degree B.Tech-M.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering from JIIT Noida.
  • Currently pursuing a Ph.D. in Computer Science at IIIT Delhi under the guidance of Prof. Ganesh Bagler.

Research Interests

  • Innovative applications of Computational Gastronomy.
  • Machine learning and deep learning for food analysis and prediction.
  • Natural language processing for recipe understanding and generation.
  • Statistical data analysis for food trends and cultural influences.

Technical Skills

Committed to staying at the forefront of technology and applying skills to tackle diverse challenges.

  • Expertise in: Machine Learning, Deep Learning, NLP, Data Science, Statistical Analysis, Generative Models, Transformers
  • Proficient in: Python, C/C++, JavaScript, HTML/CSS
  • Familiar with: SQL, NoSQL databases
  • Additional skills: Image Processing, Web Development, Data Scraping

Additional Highlights

  • Delivered talk on "Can a Computer Think like a Chef" at the Symposium on Computational Gastronomy IIIT-Delhi, December 2022.
  • Excellent communication and teamwork skills.
  • Strong work ethic and a passion for lifelong learning.
